title: "Foundation"
weight: 10
---

**Goal:** get every student driving a robot, writing their first program, and
running their first real engineering-iteration loop. Teleoperation is established
here as the command layer the rest of the course builds on.

**Who's in the room:** all new — no prior programming or robotics experience assumed.

**Programming level:** Level 0 (driving) → MakeCode blocks (modify a button, then
raw servo angles).

**Format:** three 90-minute sessions, run in order, before the modular phase opens up.
